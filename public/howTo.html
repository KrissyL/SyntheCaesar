How To Play?

sytheCaesar is a rhythm based game where you have to do 2 tasks at the same time: playing the synthesizer and shoot the enemies.

The rules of the game are fairly simple: you have to press 'A', 'S,' and 'D' keys on your keyboard at the right time and also destroy the enemies that are coming towards you on the right side of the screen.

Each successful key press shoots laser from you spaceship on the right, this is how you destroy enemies.

The game ends when your spaceship exlodes or if you completed the level.
